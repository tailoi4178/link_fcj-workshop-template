Sharing and Feedback

Overall Evaluation

1. Internship Environment

The internship environment in the FCAJ program was friendly and open. During the learning and practice process, I was able to communicate with other members when facing difficulties. The learning environment also motivated me to explore AWS and Cloud technologies further.

However, I think the program could organize more social activities or team-building sessions so that members have more opportunities to connect and communicate with each other.

2. Support from Mentors and Team Admin

During the internship, I received support from mentors and the team admin whenever I faced difficulties during the learning and practice process. Problems related to AWS or practical exercises were explained and guided so that I could research and solve them independently.

I appreciate being encouraged to research and try different solutions before receiving direct guidance. This helped me improve my self-learning and problem-solving skills.

3. Relevance to My Major

The internship content was relevant to my Information Technology major. In addition to the programming knowledge I learned at university, the program allowed me to explore Cloud Computing, AWS, and Serverless Architecture.

Through practical exercises and my personal project, I was able to combine my Backend programming knowledge with Cloud services. This was a valuable experience for my future career development.

4. Learning and Skill Development Opportunities

During the internship, I had the opportunity to learn more about AWS services and how to deploy applications in the Cloud. I also improved my skills in researching documentation, self-learning, and troubleshooting issues during practical exercises.

Besides technical knowledge, I also learned how to write technical reports, present information, and participate in AWS-related activities and events. These experiences helped me better understand a real working environment.

5. Culture and Teamwork

I experienced a positive learning and supportive environment throughout the program. Members were able to communicate and share the problems they encountered during their learning and practice.

Through group activities and AWS events, I had opportunities to connect with people who share similar interests in Cloud Computing and technology. This motivated me to continue learning and improving my skills.

6. Internship Support

The program provided learning materials and workshops that helped me have a clearer direction while learning about AWS. The practical content was organized into different topics, allowing me to gradually become familiar with Cloud services.

In addition, participating in AWS-related events and activities was another positive aspect of the program, providing me with additional knowledge and practical perspectives on Cloud technologies.

Additional Questions

What I Am Most Satisfied With

What I am most satisfied with is having the opportunity to learn about AWS and Cloud Computing through practical lessons and exercises. Previously, I mainly focused on Backend programming, but through this program, I had the opportunity to learn more about deploying and operating applications in the Cloud.

Areas for Improvement

I think the program could provide more live guidance sessions or regular Q&A sessions so that interns can discuss and resolve problems they encounter during their practical work.

In addition, more group activities or mini projects could be organized to give members more opportunities to practice and collaborate with each other.

Would I Recommend the Program to Others?

Yes. I would recommend this program to students who are studying Information Technology or are interested in Cloud Computing and AWS. The program provides learning materials and practical exercises that help beginners gradually become familiar with AWS.

In particular, the program is suitable for students who want to learn more about Cloud technologies and apply their knowledge to personal projects.

Suggestions and Future Expectations

Suggestions for Improving the Internship Experience

I suggest that the program could organize more experience-sharing sessions with AWS professionals or developers currently working in the industry. This would help interns better understand how AWS is used in real-world projects.

In addition, more project review or technical sharing sessions could be organized so that interns can receive feedback and improve their projects.

Future Participation

I would like to continue participating in AWS-related activities and programs in the future. After the internship, I plan to further study AWS, Cloud Computing, and Serverless Architecture to apply them to my personal projects and support my career development as a Backend Developer.

Additional Feedback

Overall, the internship program provided me with many valuable experiences. I not only gained more knowledge about AWS and Cloud Computing but also improved my self-learning, documentation research, and problem-solving skills.

I would like to thank the mentors, team admin, and other members for their support throughout the internship. This was a memorable experience that gave me a clearer direction for my future career development.
