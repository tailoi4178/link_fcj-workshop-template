Week 5 - Amazon Lightsail and EC2 Auto Scaling

Week 5 Objectives:

  • Understand the purpose and use cases of Amazon Lightsail.
  • Learn how Amazon Lightsail simplifies cloud infrastructure deployment.
  • Understand the concept of application scalability.
  • Learn how EC2 Auto Scaling automatically adjusts computing capacity.
  • Understand Launch Templates, Auto Scaling Groups, and Scaling Policies.
  • Practice configuring an Auto Scaling environment.

Week 5 Achievements:

  • Understood the purpose and basic architecture of Amazon Lightsail.

  • Learned the main components of Lightsail:

    • Instances
    • Networking
    • Storage
    • Databases

  • Compared Amazon Lightsail and Amazon EC2 based on:

    • Ease of use
    • Configuration complexity
    • Pricing model
    • Flexibility

  • Successfully created and configured an Amazon Lightsail Instance.

  • Learned the concept of application scalability and the importance of automatically adjusting computing resources.

  • Understood the main components of EC2 Auto Scaling:

    • Launch Template
    • Auto Scaling Group
    • Desired Capacity
    • Minimum Capacity
    • Maximum Capacity
    • Scaling Policy

  • Learned how Auto Scaling can automatically add or remove EC2 instances based on application demand.

  • Practiced creating an Auto Scaling Group and configuring scaling parameters.

  • Tested the scaling behavior of EC2 resources.

  • Improved knowledge of high availability, scalability, and resource optimization on AWS.

  • Learned the importance of monitoring resource usage and cleaning up unused resources to control AWS costs.
